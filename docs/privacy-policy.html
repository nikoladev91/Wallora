Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Wallora respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Wallora mobile application.

1. Information collected directly by Wallora

Wallora does not require users to create an account or provide personal information such as a name, email address, postal address or password.

Wallora does not directly collect or store personal information on external Wallora servers.

2. Favorites and local application data

Wallpapers added to Favorites are stored locally on the user's device. This information is used only to provide the Favorites feature and is not transmitted to Wallora servers.

3. Saving and setting wallpapers

Wallora allows users to save wallpapers to their device gallery and set images as device wallpapers. These actions occur only after the user selects the relevant function.

Wallora does not upload the user's photos, files or gallery content to external Wallora servers.

4. Advertising

Wallora uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob and its advertising partners may collect or process information such as:

  • device information,
  • advertising identifiers,
  • IP address,
  • approximate location derived from the IP address,
  • advertisement interactions,
  • diagnostic and performance data.

This information may be used to deliver, measure, personalize and improve advertisements, subject to Google's policies and the user's privacy choices.

5. European privacy choices

Users located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom or Switzerland may be shown a consent message relating to personalized advertising and data processing.

6. Children's privacy

Wallora is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

7. Third-party services

Wallora uses services provided by Google, including Google AdMob. These services operate according to their own privacy policies and terms.

8. Data security

Wallora limits direct data collection and does not maintain user accounts or an external database containing user personal information. However, no method of electronic processing or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the application changes, new features are introduced or legal requirements change. The updated date will be shown at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact: wallora.support@gmail.com
